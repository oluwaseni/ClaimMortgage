This is a prototype web site for verifying land ownerships and registering mortgages on lands. From this web, land registry can upload and update appropriate and necessary land information required by banks to perform their verification and mortgage registrations. Banks and other mortgaging institutions can confirm land information supplied by clients and further register the new mortgage with the land services after paying a token..

Also banks can also perform land title verification and register their mortgages on lands.


This web map page was built using OpenLayers API and JavaScript in order to visualize the land information on the land parcels served as WMS.
