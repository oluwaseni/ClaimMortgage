This is a prototype web site for verifying land ownership and registering mortgages on lands.

From this web, land registry can upload and update appropriate and necessary land information required by banks to perform their verification and mortgage registrations. Banks and other mortgaging institutions can confirm land information supplied by clients and further register the new mortgage with the land services after paying a token.

Also users through the services tab can make request for land title verification.
Staff can log in to a more detailed map.

This web map page was built using OpenLayers API and JavaScript in order to visualize necessary land information on land parcels served as WMS.

most popular services
  • Verifying of land title's authenticity

  • Checking and verifying the mortgage status of land
  • Registering new mortgage on land parcels
  • Updating mortgage status of land parcels
